# Wireframe Concepts: Berswara Rent

## 1. Home Page
- **Header:** Logo (Left), Nav Links (Center), CTA "Browse Catalog" (Right).
- **Hero:** High-quality image of a baby in a stroller. Text: "Premium Baby Gear for Rent. Safe. Clean. Affordable."
- **Featured Categories:** 3 Cards (Strollers, Push Walkers, Push Bikes).
- **Why Us:** 3 Icons (Hygiene First, Premium Brands, Flexible Rental).
- **Testimonials:** Short quotes from happy parents.

## 2. Catalog Page
- **Sidebar (Desktop) / Top Drawer (Mobile):** Filter by Category, Price Range, Age Group.
- **Product Grid:** 3-column grid (Desktop).
    - Card: Image, Title, Price/Week, "View Details" button.

## 3. Product Detail Page
- **Left Column:** Media Gallery (Main image/video + Thumbnails).
    - Support for multiple photos (static).
    - Support for video playback (YouTube/External link).
- **Right Column:** 
    - Breadcrumbs (Home > Catalog > Strollers).
    - Product Title & Brand.
    - Pricing Table (Weekly vs Monthly).
    - Features List (Weight limit, foldable, etc).
    - **Primary CTA:** "Check Availability on WhatsApp" (Sticky on Mobile).
- **Bottom Section:** "You might also like" (Related products).

## 4. How to Rent Page
- **Step 1:** Choose your gear.
- **Step 2:** Check availability via WhatsApp.
- **Step 3:** Confirm & Pay.
- **Step 4:** Delivery or Pick-up.
