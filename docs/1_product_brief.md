# Product Brief: Berswara Rent

## 1. Project Overview
Berswara Rent is a baby equipment rental service based in **Bandung, Jawa Barat**, focusing on mobility and developmental toys. The goal is to provide parents in the Bandung area with high-quality, clean, and safe baby gear without the high cost of ownership and storage burden.

## 2. Objective
To create a professional, trustworthy, and easy-to-navigate static website that showcases the available inventory and facilitates the rental process (initially via manual contact).

## 3. Target Audience
- New parents in Bandung looking for premium baby gear.
- Travelers or visitors to Bandung needing temporary baby equipment.
- Parents who want to "try before they buy" expensive items like strollers or push bikes.

## 4. MVP Scope (Static Website)
- **Company Information:** About us, why rent from us, hygiene standards.
- **Product Catalog:** Categories for Strollers, Push Walkers, and Push Bikes.
- **Product Details:** Basic specs, pricing per duration (weekly/monthly), multi-media support (photos & videos), and availability status.
- **Contact/Booking:** Direct link to WhatsApp or Contact Form for booking inquiries.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Clear presentation of all inventory items.
- Low friction for users to find pricing.
- High conversion from "Viewer" to "Inquiry" (WhatsApp click).

## 6. Brand Voice
- Trustworthy, Clean, Helpful, and Playful (but professional).
